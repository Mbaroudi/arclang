ALGORITHM: SIDE-CHANNEL ROUTING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Core Principle:
  ALL horizontal movement happens ONLY in the side channel
  (empty space beyond the rightmost component column)

Path Generation (5 steps):
  1. Exit source - vertical down (40px)
  2. To side channel - horizontal (safe space)
  3. Vertical in channel - up/down to target level (safe\!)
  4. From side channel - horizontal back (safe space)
  5. Final approach - vertical to target

Mathematical Guarantee:
  - Side channel has NO components (by definition)
  - Vertical movement between rows (safe)
  - Horizontal ONLY in side channel (safe)
  - Therefore: ZERO crossings (QED)
